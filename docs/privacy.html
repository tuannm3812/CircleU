Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

At Circleu, we believe self-reflection should be a safe, calm, and private experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and secure your voice reflections, transcripts, and journal entries, as well as how you maintain complete control over your data.

2. Information We Collect

Circleu is built as a local-first application. The information you create includes:

  • Voice Reflections: Audio recordings captured when you use the check-in feature.
  • Transcripts: Speech-to-text translations of your voice reflections.
  • Journal Entries: Your edited titles, emotions, private notes, and custom tags.
  • Quest and Tips Progress: Tracking data regarding active, completed, or skipped suggested growth steps.
  • Account Details: Email and display name created during onboarding.

3. How We Process and Sync Your Data

  • Local Storage: All journal entries, transcripts, and progress milestones are stored on your device first, allowing you to use the app entirely offline.
  • Cloud Backup (Firebase): When you sign up and sign in, your data is securely backed up to Cloud Firestore. Firebase Authentication secures your account.
  • AI Insights (Apple Intelligence): AI reflections are generated using on-device models when available. When local models are unsupported, a temporary secure text payload may be analyzed to generate insights. Your raw transcripts are never used for model training.

4. Data Deletion and Your Controls

We believe you should remain in complete control of your data.

  • You can edit or delete any journal entry locally at any time.
  • You can delete your entire account and all associated cloud backups directly from the Settings > Trust & Safety menu inside the app. This triggers an immediate, permanent deletion of your profile, authentication credentials, and journal entries from our database.

5. Third-Party Services

Circleu does not share, rent, or sell your private reflections, transcripts, or notes to any third-party advertising services. We only utilize Firebase (Google Cloud) as our secure cloud backend host for testing, backing up, and authenticating user data.
